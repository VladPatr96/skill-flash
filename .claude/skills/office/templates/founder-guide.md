# Founder Guide

Office is an agent company, not a chat transcript. Your job is to make the few decisions that only you can make.

## Useful Commands

- `/office <goal>`: run the company pipeline.
- `/solve <task>`: finish a one-off task without company bootstrap.
- `/memo <task>`: turn raw work into a ready issue spec.
- `/greenlight <action>`: approve an external or irreversible action.
- `/planerka`: review facts, decisions, and next work.
- `grill me`: answer only blocking questions.

## What To Expect

- Work becomes specs before execution.
- Agents may ask for approval before installs, spending, production, publishing, or external messages.
- Completed work is accepted by a verifier, not by the executor.
- Non-blocking questions are batched into standups.
- Lessons are written when a repeated failure pattern is confirmed.

## Decision Quality

Good decisions include outcome, immovable constraint, acceptable risk, default if silent, and what would reverse the decision.

## Red Flags

- The executor is also verifying.
- A task starts without a spec.
- The agent asks questions it could discover.
- A tool is installed without an offer.
- The same failure repeats without a lesson.
